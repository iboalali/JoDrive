Feasibility study:

The domain name is available for $15 at https://www.godaddy.com
The host for the website and the storage, we chose Amazon S3.

Amazon S3:
Amazon won us over because of the most good pricing and best Reliability with up to 99.999999999% durability and with 99.99% availability. It's secure, fast and  easily scalable with a few clicks, backed-up and archived for a disaster recovery. With all this, it's simple to use.[1]
There is even a free usage tier for testing with 5GB standard storage 20,000 Get Requests, 2,000 Put Requests, and 15GB of data transfer each month for one year, with a lot other benefits.[2]

Amazon Glacier is an extremely low-cost storage service data archiving and backup. it is optimized for infrequently data accessing. [4]

Reduced Redundancy Storage (RRS) is for storing non-critical, reproducible data with less redundancy than the standard storage. [5]
